Wildlife Conservation

Efforts to protect wildlife during disasters

1. Wildlife Tracking and Rescue

Monitor and track wildlife populations in disaster-prone areas using satellite data and GPS collars. Learn how to identify and report endangered species in distress, and notify conservation teams about animals in danger through the rescue alert button.

2. Safe Zones for Wildlife

Access information about safe zones for wildlife relocation during disasters. Use AI and satellite data to predict the safest migration routes for animals to avoid affected areas.

3. Conservation Guidelines and Resources

Educational content on how disasters impact wildlife. Learn about measures to preserve ecosystems and guidelines on helping wildlife during emergencies, such as creating wildlife corridors and avoiding disturbances.

4. Post-Disaster Habitat Restoration

Suggest reforestation and habitat restoration projects post-disaster. Collaborate with conservation groups to recruit volunteers for wildlife habitat rebuilding efforts.

5. Wildlife-Friendly Evacuation Routes

Designated evacuation routes that consider wildlife migration and movement patterns. Learn how to navigate these routes to avoid disturbing animals during evacuation efforts.
